About Us

Frequently asked questions

SoliFood is a food sharing platform designed to connect individuals and businesses with surplus food to those in need within our community. It aims to address food surplus while fostering community connections.

SoliFood is open to anyone who wants to share surplus food or access food donations. This includes individuals, restaurants, grocery stores, and other food-related businesses, as well as individuals and families in need of food assistance.

SoliFood encourages donors to provide accurate information about the condition and expiration date of surplus food items. Users can also leave reviews and ratings based on their experience with food donations, helping to maintain accountability and quality standards.

Yes, SoliFood is completely free to use for both donors and recipients. There are no fees associated with posting surplus food items or accessing food donations.
